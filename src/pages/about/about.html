No History Chat application!

Version : 1.0.3

Simple chat application that protects your right to private chatting!

We don't store any of your messages, just the ones that are sent to you while you were offline. You can even choose to turn this feature off.

Your email is used for authentication and account confirmation. Your data is not shared with anyone, just used internally.

It is best to choose password that you do not use anywhere else, choose a password that is unique to this app!

Your device informations ( uuid,serial number and manufacturer ) are stored for security purposes only

That is it. That is all the informations we gather.

Keep in mind, this application was made by a single developer, so please be understanding of any bugs you might encounter.
